About US

Our Real Story

At SYNECAD, we believe that financial solutions should not be exclusively offered by traditional banking institutions. Our platform fosters synergy by empowering individuals to contribute toward their own financial well-being and that of others within small to medium-sized groups, starting with a minimum of 5 people. We are committed to building a digital community where collaborative financing is driven by shared commitment, enabling members to look toward financial affluence without the complexities of traditional bank loans.

Mission And Vision

Our Mission & Vission

Our mission is to build flexible, cooperative financial communities that make self-funding easily accessible and sustainable, free from conventional restrictions. Our vision is to become the leading platform for digital, collaborative financing, connecting individuals around the world to achieve their goals together.

A Unique Financing Mechanism

Our Mission & Vission

SYNECAD stands out with an innovative mechanism that strengthens bonds within small communities. We help individuals pool their resources to fund their projects and meet their needs, where each member becomes a central pillar in the group’s success. This alternative approach to traditional finance highlights the value of collaboration as an economic solution based on trust and responsibility.

The concept of SYNECAD emerged from an urgent need to transform social bonds into meaningful, practical steps that help individuals overcome financial challenges in innovative and sustainable ways. Through our platform, we support individuals in transitioning from isolated financial approaches to collective action, making SYNECAD not only a tool for funding but also a digital experience that promotes the values of mutual support and shared commitment in today’s complex world

How SYNECAD Works

How SYNECAD Works ?

SYNECAD offers two self-funding models tailored to meet diverse needs:

In this model, group members contribute a fixed amount each month. In each funding cycle, one member of the group receives the total pooled amount until each member has had a turn. The Fixed Contribution Model provides regular funding without the need for costly loans.

Examples of SYNECAD Funding Models

Fixed Contribution Model

Consider a funding group of 5 members: John, Emily, Michael, Sarah, and David. Each member contributes a fixed amount of 100 euros per month. The funding period lasts for 5 months, during which one member receives the total pooled amount each month. Therefore, each member contributes 100 euros per month, making a total monthly pool of 500 euros.

Monthly Pool

Month Member Receiving the Amount Monthly Contribution per Member Total Monthly Pool Payout to Member
1 John 100 euros 500 euros 500 euros
2 Emily 100 euros 500 euros 500 euros
3 Michael 100 euros 500 euros 500 euros
4 Sarah 100 euros 500 euros 500 euros
5 David 100 euros 500 euros 500 euros

By the end of the cycle, each member has contributed 500 euros and received 500 euros, ensuring equal funding for all members.

Flexible Contribution Model

In this example, we have another group of 5 members: Jessica, Tom, Alice, Robert, and Linda. Each member has chosen a different monthly contribution amount ranging from 50 to 150 euros. The funding period lasts 5 months, and each month, one member receives the full payout based on their total contributions for the entire period

Member Contributions and Payouts

Member Monthly Contribution Total Contribution (Monthly x 5 Months) Month of Payout Payout Amount
Jessica 50 euros 250 euros Month 1 250 euros
Tom 100 euros 500 euros Month 2 500 euros
Alice 75 euros 375 euros Month 3 375 euros
Robert 150 euros 750 euros Month 4 750 euros
Linda 125 euros 625 euros Month 5 625 euros

In this example, the payout amount varies according to each member's contribution, offering greater flexibility for members and enhancing cooperation in line with their financial capabilities.
