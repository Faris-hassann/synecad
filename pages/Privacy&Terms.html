Introduction: Privacy and Security

SYNECAD is designed to encourage individuals to cooperate and commit collectively to overcoming financial challenges together. By joining the platform, users are expected to take their commitments seriously toward one another. This collaborative approach not only provides easy, unconditional funding at significantly lower costs compared to traditional financial options but also fosters the creation of close-knit communities bound by mutual trust, rules, and shared responsibilities

The concept of financial rounds through collective contributions ensures that benefits are shared equally among all participants, while the risks of commitment are also fairly distributed. This model empowers users to achieve financial success and stability while building strong connections within their groups.

1. Overview of Services

SYNECAD provides a digital platform that allows users to:

  • Create private financial groups with individuals they know personally.
  • Establish financial rounds where group members contribute a pre-agreed monthly amount and receive the total collected amount in turn.
  • Facilitate contributions and payouts while deducting a 7% service fee (inclusive of transaction costs).

Our platform acts as an intermediary to facilitate these operations but does not guarantee participant behavior or completion of financial rounds.

Each user has a digital wallet where funds can be deposited at any time. The platform automatically collects the agreed monthly contribution for active rounds from the user's wallet on the specified date
